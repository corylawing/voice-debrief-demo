The AI only knows what you say

Mention these out loud and the record fills in accurately. Skip one and it simply won't be captured.

How you interacted — in person, virtual, or telephone
Who you spoke to — names and roles
What was discussed
Any products or demos presented
Any evidence shared
The outcome
Any pain points
Follow-up dates

Say it out loud

Small habits that make the record sharper.

Name people
"I met Dr. Ana Novak, the orthodontist, and her staff."
Names and roles let it link the right people and set the contact's position on convert.
Give real dates
"Following up next Tuesday."
A concrete date beats "soon" — it books the follow-up and fills the next step.
Name the product
"Gave a demo of DentalMonitoring and Observation Protocols."
Specific products land in the record and sharpen the opportunity signal.
Want it converted? Say so
"I want to convert this lead."
An explicit ask triggers the convert and opens the opportunity, every time.
